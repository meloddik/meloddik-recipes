Lemon Cupcakes

Description

Embark on a delightful baking adventure and treat your taste buds to a burst of sunshine with our recipe for the tastiest Lemon Cupcakes. These delectable confections marry the light, airy texture of a perfectly baked cupcake with the zesty brightness of fresh lemons, creating a harmonious symphony of flavors. Each bite is a journey through layers of moist, lemon-infused cake topped with a velvety swirl of citrus-kissed frosting. Whether you're a baking enthusiast or a novice in the kitchen, our easy-to-follow recipe promises to deliver cupcakes that are not only visually appealing but also irresistibly scrumptious. Get ready to infuse your day with a sweet and tangy indulgence that is sure to leave a lasting impression.

Ingredients

For the Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons

For the Lemon Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Steps

Preheat the Oven:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)

Prepare the Cupcake Batter:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt
  2. In a separate bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk, and mixing until just combined
  5. Fold in the lemon zest and juice

Fill Cupcake Liners:

  1. Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners
  2. Spoon the batter into the liners, filling each about two-thirds full

Bake:

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean

Cool Completely:

  1. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely

Prepare the Lemon Frosting:

  1. In a bowl, beat together the softened butter, powdered sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest until smooth and creamy

Frost the Cupcakes:

  1. Once the cupcakes are completely cooled, frost them with the lemon frosting using a piping bag or a spatula

Garnish and Serve:

  1. Optionally, garnish the cupcakes with additional lemon zest or a slice of lemon
  2. Serve and enjoy the irresistibly tasty Lemon Cupcakes
