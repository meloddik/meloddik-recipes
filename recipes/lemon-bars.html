Lemon Bars

Description

Indulge your taste buds in a burst of citrusy delight with our irresistible Lemon Bars recipe. Perfectly balancing the zesty freshness of lemons with the sweet decadence of a buttery crust, these bars are a harmonious symphony of flavors and textures. Each bite offers a tangy and refreshing sensation, making them an ideal treat for any occasion. Whether you're a seasoned baker or a kitchen novice, our straightforward recipe ensures that you can effortlessly whip up these lemon-infused delights. Get ready to elevate your dessert game with the perfect blend of tartness and sweetness in every luscious square.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Lemons

Steps

Prepare the Crust

  1. Combine 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup of powdered sugar, and a pinch of salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut in 1/2 cup of cold, unsalted butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Press the mixture into a greased 9x9-inch baking pan to form an even crust.
  4. Bake the crust in a preheated oven at 350째F (175째C) for 15-20 minutes or until lightly golden.

Make the Lemon Filling

  1. In a separate bowl, whisk together 1 cup of granulated sugar, 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour, and 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder.
  2. Add 2 large eggs and the juice and zest of 2 lemons. Whisk until smooth and well combined.

Bake the Bars

  1. Pour the lemon filling over the pre-baked crust.
  2. Bake in the oven at 350째F (175째C) for 20-25 minutes or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.

Cool and Chill

  1. Allow the lemon bars to cool completely in the pan on a wire rack.
  2. Once cooled, refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until fully chilled.

Serve and Enjoy

  1. Dust the top with powdered sugar before cutting into squares.
  2. Serve chilled and savor the delightful combination of tangy lemon and sweet buttery crust.
